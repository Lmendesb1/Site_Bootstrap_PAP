About Ardrumino

The musical innovation

The Ardrumino was designed according to real musical drums.

Through an Arduino YÚN and some integrated piezos the user is able to play different sounds and create his own beat!

Piezos are responsible for transforming mechanical energy into electrical energy, thus emitting a sound when played.

Our Creator

Idealist and founder of the project.

The Ardrumino project was founded by Lucas Mendes, a programming student in Braga, Portugal.

Lucas showed interest in music and technology since he was a child.

"This project was thought for a long time by me, I love music and I am determined by computing. When this opportunity arose to put my project into practice I felt that I could not waste it. This is an achievement for me!" said Lucas.
